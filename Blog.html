Messi vs Ronaldo: The Eternal GOAT Debate.

Few debates in sports spark as much passion, loyalty, and fire as Lionel Messi vs Cristiano Ronaldo. For over 15 years, the two have dominated football, redefining greatness and pushing each other to unimaginable heights. Both are legends, both are record-breakers, and both are global icons—but when it comes to the GOAT (Greatest of All Time) question, fans split into two camps.
